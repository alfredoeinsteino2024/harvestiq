INCIDENT REPORT // The Problem

Nigerian farmers lose 40% of their harvest — not from bad farming, but bad information.

In the 72 hours after harvest, most smallholder farmers have no idea what the market price is, how long they can safely store their produce, or who is buying today. HarvestIQ solves all three.

📉

No price visibility

Farmers sell to middlemen at 40% below market rate because they don't know the real price at nearby markets.

🍅

Produce spoilage

Without storage guidance, up to 50% of tomatoes and peppers rot before they reach buyers.

🤝

No buyer access

Farmers have no direct way to reach buyers, processors or aggregators — they depend entirely on middlemen.

📵

Smartphone barrier

Existing agritech solutions require smartphones and internet. 99% of farmers only have basic feature phones.
