The Problem
Farmers lose 40% of their harvest — not from bad farming, but bad information.

In the 72 hours after harvest, most smallholder farmers have no idea what the market price is, how long they can safely store their produce, or who is buying today. HarvestIQ solves all three.

📉
No price visibility
Farmers sell to middlemen at 40% below market rate because they don't know the real price at nearby markets.
🍅
Produce spoilage
Without storage guidance, up to 50% of tomatoes and peppers rot before reaching buyers.
🤝
No buyer access
Farmers have no direct way to reach buyers or processors — entirely dependent on middlemen.
📵
Smartphone barrier
Existing agritech requires smartphones and internet. 99% of farmers only have basic feature phones.
